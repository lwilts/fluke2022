Location


Merchant Taylors' Hall is a beautiful Elizabethan building in the bustling, historic Square Mile. It's home to one of the great twelve livery companies of the City of London. Located on Threadneedle Street, it's nestled between the grand old buildings of the Bank of England and the Royal Exchange, and the shining skyscrapers of Bishopsgate.

Getting there

Parking is prety limited - we recommend getting a taxi, or the Underground to Bank station or Liverpool Street. If you do need to park nearby, check Parkopedia to see available car parks.

Dress code

Black tie - your choice of dinner suit (tuxedo) or a black suit with bow tie, or a long dress. It's what the location deserves!
