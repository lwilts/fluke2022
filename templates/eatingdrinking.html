If you need inspiration, here are some of our favourite places.

Eating

Smokestak in Shoreditch is one of our favourite places for delicious meaty goodness.

Dean Street Townhouse is a fun place for great British food in sophisticated surrounds, in the culinary heart of London's West End, Soho.

Drinking

The Ned is a fun place for a classy drink in a former banking hall (just down the road from the wedding venue) - it's also a hotel if you fancy splashing out. There's often live music too.

Not with any connection to the brother of the bride, St Swithin's Wine Bar (open Mon-Fri) is tucked away on the lane of the same name.

In Soho, Cafe Boheme is a French-style bistro with excellent cocktails.

For a late night cocktail, Discount Suit Company is a speakeasy bar with fantastic drinks.
